About MessageBoard

AI-Powered Message Standardisation for Schools

Our Purpose

MessageBoard helps teachers create professional, standardised pastoral care messages quickly and efficiently using AI technology.

How It Works

Input Your Message

Enter your observations about student behaviour in your own words.

AI Standardisation

Our AI transforms your input into professional, appropriate language.

Review & Use

Review the standardised message and copy it for your communications.

Store & Track

All your messages are saved for easy reference and tracking.

Key Features

  • Smart Categorisation: Positive and negative behaviour tracking
  • School Policy Integration: Matches messages to your school's behaviour guide
  • Professional Tone: Ensures all messages maintain appropriate language
  • Easy Copying: One-click copy of standardised messages
  • Date Filtering: Find messages by specific dates
  • Customizable Colors: Personalize your dashboard experience

Who It's For

MessageBoard is designed for teachers and educators who need to communicate with parents and administrators about student behaviour, achievements, and concerns in a professional manner.
